Who We Are

M&A Cleaning Service is a small housekeeping team proudly serving the vibrant city of Los Angeles. With over 15 years of professional cleaning experience, we bring reliability, attention to detail, and a personalized touch to every home we care for. Our long list of satisfied clients and glowing references speak to the quality and consistency of our work.

Your comfort, safety, and satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do. At M&A Cleaning Service, we approach every home with care and professionalism, treating your space with the same respect and attention we would our own. We are committed to delivering a consistently exceptional cleaning experience — one that leaves your home refreshed and your mind at ease. Understanding that every household has unique needs and routines, we offer flexible scheduling options to accommodate your lifestyle. Whether you prefer weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly service, we’ll work with you to set up a recurring cleaning plan that fits seamlessly into your schedule.

Our Services

Our residential housekeeping services cover all the essential areas of your home, including thorough cleaning of bathrooms, bedrooms, kitchens, and interior windows.
